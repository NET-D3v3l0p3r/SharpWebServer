DOWNLOAD PHP http://windows.php.net/download#php-7.1
AND UNZIP IT INTO \bin\Debug\Configuration\plugin


YOU CAN CHANGE PHP-FOLDER BY CHANGING 
IN INI-FILE FOLLOWING LINE:
:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:
[PlugIn]
PlugInFolder=Configuration\plugin
PlugInExtensions=php;
PlugInApplications=php->Configuration\plugin\php\php.exe|
:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:=:
